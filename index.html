Hi, I am Yi Qu

I love coding, and classical music.

I started my PhD research at the department of computer science in Illinois Institute of Technology in the year 2019. My research interests focus on the theory and application of Artificial Intelligence, especially in time series analysis and natural language processing. When not in academia, I play classical piano and classical guitar. If time and weather permits, I take astronomical photographs.

When the COVID pandemic hit the world, I withdrew from IIT due to the lack of funding. Then, I witnessed the world being split apart. However, science and music never take sides. Regardless of race, gender, or cultural background, the profound research results from the scientists and the revelatory masterpieces from the artists bring people together.

I learn from those beautiful minds.
